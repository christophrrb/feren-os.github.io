This website stores two Cookies locally on your computer: One in order to remember which search engine you used last, so that upon the next time the page is loaded, the search engine will be automatically chosen depending on the last used search engine, and another to determine your Custom Background Image. If you do not want this behaviour then your plan of action is to use an alternative Start Page instead or disable Cookies. However, please note that we do not retrieve these cookies for our own purposes, but instead they are simply used and edited by the website itself, whenever needed, to deliver a better experience, meaning that these cookies are only used locally and are never sent to the developer or anyone else by the usage of this Start Page.
