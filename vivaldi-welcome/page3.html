Get your Privacy in check

Having agreed to the Vivaldi End User License Agreement and the Vivaldi Privacy Policy, you are probably wondering what Privacy is available to Vivaldi Users by default, as well as what privacy settings are set by default. Every Privacy Setting in Vivaldi is set to its default setting at the time in Feren OS but 'Search Suggestions in Address Field', which comes pre-enabled in Feren OS. Therefore, I'm expecting even just one user to be unhappy with this and want to disable this option. Here's how to disable it:

egg

① Go into the ⚙Settings Cog at the bottom-left of your Vivaldi Window

② In the window that has now opened, go to 'Privacy' (on the left)

③ Tweak the Privacy Settings to your liking... it's a browser for you, don't be afraid to customise it your way.
