Get Bookmarks from other browsers

Vivaldi, just like any other web browser available, easily allows you to take the bookmarks and more from other web browsers and put them onto Vivaldi to get you started faster. If you have any other browsers with bookmarks on this user account before launching Vivaldi for the first time, you can import the bookmarks from these browsers by doing the following:

egg

① Go into the Vivaldi Menu (Vivaldi Icon by default) at the top-left of your Vivaldi Window

② In the menu that has now opened, go to 'File' > 'Import Bookmarks and Settings...'

③ Choose a Browser to get bookmarks from, and fill out the other steps if neccessary, and then press 'Start Import'.
