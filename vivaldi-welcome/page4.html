Sync your data

Vivaldi is a huge customisability playground of a browser when you open it up, therefore I'm sure you don't want to lose your configurations, passwords, and all that other stuff you store in your Vivaldi Browser if anything was to go wrong, do you? Fortunately for you, the Vivaldi Team has introduced the ability to synchronise all your Vivaldi Settings, Passwords, Accounts, etc, via your Vivaldi.net Account.

egg

① Go into the ⚙Settings Cog at the bottom-left of your Vivaldi Window

② In the window that has now opened, go to 'Sync' (on the left)

③ Sign up for a Vivaldi.net account if you haven't already, and then log into your Vivaldi.net Account, and sync away!
