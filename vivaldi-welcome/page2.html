Choosing Start Pages

As well as having support for all the Chrome Extensions in Vivaldi and Start Page Extension support, Vivaldi in Feren OS also comes pre-featured with a Start Page built just for Vivaldi with Feren OS. However, if you do not like it, whether that be due to its lack of customisability or you prefer the standard Vivaldi Start Page, you can change it by doing the following:

egg

① Go into the ⚙Settings Cog at the bottom-left of your Vivaldi Window

② In the window that has now opened, go to 'Tabs' (on the left)

③ Under 'New Tab Page', choose 'Start Page'. If you want to revert at any time, either choose 'Homepage' from there, or if you've changed the Home Page, choose 'Specific Page' and set it to 'https://feren-os.github.io/start-page/' (without the quotes).
