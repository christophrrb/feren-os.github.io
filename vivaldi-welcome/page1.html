Make it yours

Vivaldi lets you choose between any colour scheme and general look and feel of the Vivaldi Browser, helping to make it yours.

egg

In Feren OS, you can also choose between some completely custom Vivaldi Themes made by me, The Feren OS Developer, being 'Edgy', 'Ow The Edge', 'Classic Light, 'Classic Dark', 'Feren OS' and 'Feren OS Dark', as well as the Standard Vivaldi Themes available out of the box to all Vivaldi users, and not just those using Feren OS.

To Manage your Themes:

① Go into the ⚙Settings Cog at the bottom-left of your Vivaldi Window

② In the window that has now opened, go to 'Themes' (on the left)

③ Select, manage, or create your own theme(s) to your content, the possibilities are yours to find.
